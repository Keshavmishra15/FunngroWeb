About Funngro

At Funngro, we believe in empowering the next generation. Our mission is to enable teenagers to become financially independent and responsible citizens by connecting them with real-world freelancing opportunities.

Founded in 2021 by educators, tech enthusiasts, IIM alumni, finance experts, and most importantly, loving parents, Funngro bridges the gap between smart teenagers and forward-thinking companies.
