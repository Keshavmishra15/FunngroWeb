About Funngro

Funngro connects smart teenagers with companies to work on real-world projects, fostering financial independence and skill development. Founded by Payal Jain and Anik Jain, Funngro is shaping the future workforce.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up and create your profile.
  2. Select projects that match your interests and skills.
  3. Work, collaborate, and learn real-world skills.
  4. Get paid and receive certificates for your achievements.
